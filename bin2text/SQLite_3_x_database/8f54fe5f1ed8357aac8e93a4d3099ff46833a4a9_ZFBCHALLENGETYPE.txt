Every day of the challenge, your team’s daily steps will be collected and averaged. Those daily averages will be added to create an overall total score. The team with the highest step score wins.

Here’s an example of how we add things up:
Your Team Average Day 1 = 10,500 Steps
Your Team Average Day 2 = 11,250 Steps
Total Score for Your Team So Far = 21,750

WHO
This challenge includes all participants from the teams participating in challenge. Participants can still join the challenge after it starts, but those who haven't contributed any steps in a given day will not be included in their team’s average for that day. Only steps tracked by a Fitbit tracker or Fitbit MobileTrack since you joined the challenge will be counted, so activities that are manually logged will not be included.

TIMEZONE
Challenges begin at 12:00am on the selected start date in the timezone of the activating person, and will stop at 11:59pm on the chosen end date. Results for each day will be fully calculated after all participants in all timezones have synced their devices. After the challenge ends, all participants will have additional time to sync their devices before the results are tallied.

PRIVACY
Participants in your team can see your profile photo, posted messages and daily steps in the challenge.
